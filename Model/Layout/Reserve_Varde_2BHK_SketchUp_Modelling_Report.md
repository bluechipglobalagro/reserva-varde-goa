## 5. Architectural Features
- **Courtyard Plunge Pool:** 1.4 × 3.0 m, Z=-1100 mm with wave water texture
- **Glass Corridors:** Seamless 1100 mm wide glazed walkway crossing the courtyard
- **Solar Energy:** 6 solar panels on the West Wing roof
- **Forest Deck:** Multi-level hardwood deck extending South into the jungle
- **Pergola:** Double-timber frame shading the front verandah

## 6. Site Context Details
- 1-acre landscaped boundary with gravel arrival and parking courts (2-car capacity)
- Bio-septic tank location, rainwater harvesting tank, and solar inverter zone marked
- Dense forest tree buffer (Plumerias, Mangoes, Palms) modeled around the boundary

## 7. Compliance and Verification Required
- **Structural Engineering:** Verification of helical screw pile bearing capacity in South Goa lateritic soil, container steel reinforcement around the 4.2m sliding door cutout on C1, and roof diaphragm framing.
- **Monsoon Detailing:** Rainwater gutter discharge volume, flashing around the glass connector corridors, and courtyard paving drainage slope.
- **MEP Coordination:** Septic system/STP sizing, electrical solar tie-in, and freshwater feed lines to the detached C7 utility unit.
