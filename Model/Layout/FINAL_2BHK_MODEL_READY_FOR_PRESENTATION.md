## 4. Remaining Manual Checks & Verification
Before commencing on-site grading or modular manufacturing in Goa, the developer and architectural teams must cross-reference:

1. **Topographic Slope Audit:** Confirm that the Low (-200mm), Mid (0mm), and High (+250mm) terracing coordinates precisely reflect the natural contour levels of the actual 1-acre forest plot.
2. **Structural Foundation Piles:** Design bored concrete columns to anchor the heavy red laterite plinths and modular steel castings through Goan clay soils.
3. **Monsoon Hydrology Calculations:** Certify that the $100\text{mm}$ continuous edge gutters and $80\text{mm}$ downpipes safely manage peak monsoonal downpours ($347\text{ Liters/minute}$) and divert water to swales.
4. **Wind-Load Safety Certification:** Verify that the low-profile solar panel frames weld directly to container corner headers to resist peak $50\text{ m/s}$ monsoonal/cyclonic wind gusts (BIS IS-875 standard).
5. **Municipal Clearances:** Secure CRZ setbacks and Panchayat approvals for the 1,200 Liters/day Bio-Digester STP placement and greywater subsurface irrigation lines.
