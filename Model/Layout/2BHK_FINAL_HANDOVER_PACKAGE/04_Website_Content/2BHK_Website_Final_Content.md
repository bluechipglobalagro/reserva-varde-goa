## 3. Ecological & Monsoon-Ready Systems Copy

### Section Title
> "Engineered for the Goan Monsoon. Designed for Self-Sufficiency."

### Section Subtext
> "Sustainable luxury means building in absolute harmony with local climates. Reserva Varde Goa is engineered to withstand intense South Goan rainfall while maintaining a zero-carbon, off-grid footprint."

### Bullet Features Grid

* **Off-Grid Solar Energy:**  
  *"6 high-efficiency monocrystalline PV panels ($3.3\text{ kWp}$) mounted on low-profile bronze racks. Tilted at a gentle $1.2^\circ$ (2% slope) to allow rapid storm runoff while keeping wind-uplift coefficients extremely low under cyclonic wind loads."*
* **Continuous Edge Gutters:**  
  *"Custom $100\text{mm}$ bronze-profile gutters running along the roof edges of all three wings. Connected to four vertical $80\text{mm}$ bronze downpipes, discharging peak downpours ($347\text{ Liters/minute}$) effortlessly."*
* **Monsoon Plinth Drains:**  
  *"A continuous $300\text{mm}$ wide river gravel drainage strip wrapping the base of all modular plinths. Instantly absorbs roof runoff and prevents mud splashback against the vertical timber facade slats."*
* **Raised Plinth Masonry:**  
  *"The entire villa is elevated **450mm** off the forest floor on solid bored concrete columns wrapped in native red laterite masonry. Prevents moisture ingress from damp Goan forest clay."*
* **Bio-Digester Wastewater Treatment:**  
  *"A decentralized, zero-energy three-chamber anaerobic Bio-Digester STP unit ($1,200\text{ L/day}$ capacity) completely screened behind vertical timber screen structures. Safely recycles greywater for subsurface jungle soil fertilization."*
* **Rainwater Harvesting Cistern:**  
  *"A massive $10,000\text{ Liter}$ underground rainwater harvesting cylinder equipped with a primary leaf trap and physical filtration system to secure water self-sufficiency."*
