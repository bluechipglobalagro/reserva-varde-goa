### C. How to Import and Setup inside Lumion
1. Launch **Lumion 12+**. Create a new scene using the **Tropical / Forest Hills** template.
2. Click **Import**, load:  
   `C:/Users/INTEL/Desktop/GOA/WEBSITE/Model/Layout/2BHK_FINAL_HANDOVER_PACKAGE/03_Lumion_Export_Package/Reserva_Varde_2BHK_Lumion_Export.dae`

4. Refer to `Lumion_Material_Map.md` to map `RV_` prefixed materials to realistic 3D textures.
5. Place active reflection planes on pool, reflecting channel, and C1 glass windows.
6. Replace background plant blocks with Lumion coconut palms, banana trees, and tall teak trees.
7. Configure spotlights ($2700\text{K}$) and set path light heads to emissive power **20.0**.

## ⚖️ 6. Professional Architect & Engineering Certification Note

> [!IMPORTANT]  
> All drawings, models, and technical parameters developed in this package represent high-fidelity concept designs by Bluechip Global Agro. Before commencing site grading, foundation pouring, or container modular assembly in South Goa, the developer must submit this package to a licensed local Goan architectural and structural engineering office of record for a professional review of:
> 1. **Contour & Geotechnical Piling:** Bored concrete foundation piles must be calculated to anchor the heavy red laterite plinths and modular steel castings through Goan clay forest soils.
> 2. **Cyclonic Wind-Load Certification:** Solar panels mounts and container sloped roofs must be certified under BIS IS-875 (Part 3) wind-load calculations to safely withstand peak $50\text{ m/s}$ (180 km/h) monsoonal wind-lift.
> 3. **Hydrology & Runoff Capacity:** Gutters and bioswales must be audited to ensure complete, rapid monsoonal drainage ($347\text{ Liters/minute}$ peak load).
> 4. **Municipal Clearances:** Complete CRZ forest setback mapping and Panchayat building sanctions must be formally secured for the bio-digester sewage treatment and underground rainwater harvest cylinder layouts.
