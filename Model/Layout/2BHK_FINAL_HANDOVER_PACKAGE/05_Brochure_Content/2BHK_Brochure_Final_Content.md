### 🛠️ Green Engineering Systems

| Eco Feature | Engineering Specification | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Off-Grid Solar** | 6 Monocrystalline PV panels ($3.3\text{ kWp}$) on low-profile bronze racks. | Power self-sufficiency; low-profile sloped at $1.2^\circ$ for wind safety. |
| **Continuous Gutters** | Custom $100\text{mm}$ bronze-profile gutters and four $80\text{mm}$ downpipes. | Direct peak rainwater runoff ($347\text{ L/min}$) safely away from walls. |
| **Monsoon Drains** | $300\text{mm}$ wide river gravel drainage strips wrapping plinth bases. | Prevent mud splashback against the teak timber slats. |
| **Raised Plinth** | Elevated **450mm** on concrete columns wrapped in red laterite masonry. | Prevents damp Goan soil moisture ingress. |
| **Bio-STP digester** | Zero-energy three-chamber anaerobic Bio-Digester STP ($1,200\text{ L/day}$). | Safely recycles greywater for subsurface garden irrigation. |
