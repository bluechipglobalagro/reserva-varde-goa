## Slide 4: The 2BHK Architectural Concept
*   **Slide Design:** High-level architectural focus. Features the aerial three-quarter perspective highlighting the U-shaped module configuration framing the courtyard.
*   **Visual Element:** *[Reserve_Varde_2BHK_Aerial_View.png](file:///c:/Users/INTEL/Desktop/GOA/WEBSITE/Model/Layout/Reserve_Varde_2BHK_Final_Renders/Reserve_Varde_2BHK_Aerial_View.png)*
*   **Slide Copy:**
    *   *Headline:* **The U-Shaped Spatial Grid.**
    *   *Core Pillars:*
        *   **Seven Container Modules:** Six 40ft High Cube containers forming the public and private wings, plus one detached 20ft utility block to isolate noise and vibrations.
        *   **Courtyard-Centric:** Frames a private, open-to-sky central courtyard, sheltering the outdoor space from high winds while acting as a natural light well.
        *   **Single Ground-Level Plane:** Keeps the entire villa wind-safe and accessible, eliminating vertical vertical load hazards.

## Slide 8: Technical Specs & Self-Sustaining Utilities
*   **Slide Design:** Clean, technical grid. Displays the roof aerial showing solar panel orientations and gutters.
*   **Visual Element:** *[Reserve_Varde_2BHK_Roof_Solar_View.png](file:///c:/Users/INTEL/Desktop/GOA/WEBSITE/Model/Layout/Reserve_Varde_2BHK_Final_Renders/Reserve_Varde_2BHK_Roof_Solar_View.png)*
*   **Slide Copy:**
    *   *Headline:* **Sustainable Performance.**
    *   *Specs:*
        *   **Wind-Safe Solar:** 6 PV panels mounted on fixed, low-profile rails, sloped at a gentle $1.2^{\circ}$ grade for optimal South wind-shedding and rainwater runoff.
        *   **Rainwater Cistern:** High-capacity collection tank collecting roof runoff for landscape irrigation.
        *   **Decentralized Bio-Digester:** Hidden, landscaped bio-digester utility box that naturally processes blackwater on-site.
        *   **Monsoon Defense:** Raised $450\text{mm}$ plinths, deep $1.0\text{m}$ roofs, volumetric drip edges, and bronze flashings.
