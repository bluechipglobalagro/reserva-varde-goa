The Vision

Not a plotted scheme.
A managed forest estate.

Reserva Varde Goa is not a villa project and not a layout of plots. It is a single living valley — conserved, low-density and stewarded — where each owner holds one acre of forest within a community designed around privacy, wellness and the long horizon.

Conservation comes first. Built footprint is deliberately small. The land remains the asset.

Legal & Due Diligence

Transparency is the first amenity

Every estate progresses through a documentation-led framework. We publish the checklist rather than conceal it.

  • Title deed
  • Ownership chain
  • Form I & XIV
  • Mutation records
  • Survey plan
  • DSLR-certified plan
  • Encumbrance certificate
  • Zoning certificate
  • Regional Plan classification
  • TCP approval
  • Land conversion
  • Panchayat construction licence
  • Forest status
  • ESZ / sanctuary proximity
  • Road access legality
  • Waterbody buffers
  • Environmental permissions
  • Goa RERA applicability
  • Subdivision / layout approval
  • Sale deed / agreement structure
